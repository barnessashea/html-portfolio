The Best New Movies of 2023

My Top 3 Movies of the summer.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.3

I love this movie because of the amazing graphic details and the solid storyline. This movie is definitely a step up from the first two volumes.

The Mother

The Mother is hands down one of my favorites because of the non stop action. I love a good thriller with plenty of guns.

They Cloned Tyrone

I love this movie because it's technically a documentary of what's going on here in the U.S.
